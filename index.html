Frequently asked questions

What makes TaaPlay different?
Local-language, play-based content designed for South African families and ECD practitioners, with low-data delivery and CAPS-aware guidance—all in one place.
Will TaaPlay be free?
Early access is free. A core free tier will remain after launch. Optional premium features for advanced content and tools may follow for those who need them.
When does early access open and how will I get access?
We’re rolling out invites in small waves. We prioritise a mix of roles, provinces, and preferred languages from the waitlist. Invitations are sent by email (and, later, optional WhatsApp alerts). When selected, you’ll receive setup instructions and a starter pack.
Which devices and connectivity are supported?
TaaPlay is mobile-first and works in modern browsers on Android and iOS, and on recent desktop browsers. It’s designed for low-data use; most experiences load quickly on 3G/4G. Limited offline reading for core activities is on the roadmap.
How can ECD centres and NGOs participate?
Centres and programmes can join via the waitlist—select “Centre Owner/Principal” or “NGO/Programme Manager” as your role. We prioritise diverse provinces and languages. Early-access partners receive a starter pack and short onboarding tips; for pilots, we may share a simple MOU and feedback form.
How does TaaPlay use AI?
AI helps surface age-appropriate activities, suggest language variants, and personalise recommendations. Human review guides all content. We do not require children’s personal data for the waitlist, and we follow POPIA. You can opt out of personalised suggestions at any time.
